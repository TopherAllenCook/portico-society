# Verve MD — Instantly Cold Email Sequence

The "shotgun" that runs in parallel to Sam's calls and the Loom pipe bombs. Paste each email into the matching step of the Instantly campaign. Voice rules: short, human, opens a loop, no dashes, no exclamations, no emojis, no invented numbers. The email's job is to get a reply, not to close.

## Merge fields

| Field | Source |
|---|---|
| `{{first_name}}` | lead contact first name |
| `{{company}}` | `clinic_name` (business name) |
| `{{city}}` / `{{trade}}` | lead `city` / `specialty` |
| `{{audit_finding}}` | `outbound_leads.audit_finding` — one prospect-safe sentence generated by the ICP scorer at enrichment time. Always present by export. Map this column to the `{{audit_finding}}` custom variable in Instantly. |

> Map every column to the matching Instantly variable on import. If `{{audit_finding}}` is ever blank for a lead, the scorer fell back to its safe default; the line still reads cleanly.

## Deployment notes

- **Deliverability first.** Send from secondary domains, not vervemd.com. Keep each inbox under ~30 to 40 sends/day, warm them 2 to 4 weeks before real volume, and confirm SPF/DKIM/DMARC on every sending domain. Volume target is quality over Sabri's literal 400/day; the audit-led motion is higher-touch.
- **Sequencing.** Per Sabri's own order, this validates which angle pulls before any paid spend. Watch which of the four hooks (invisible test, missed call, stop renting, plain-English system) earns the most "yes," then fund that one as a Meta ad.
- **Sister assets.** Loom pipe bombs: `public/pipe-bomb-guide.html`. Cold calls: `public/samalee-cold-calling-guide.html`. Same four hooks across all of them.
