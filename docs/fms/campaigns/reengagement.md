# Re-engagement Campaign

**Target:** C-tier and disengaged current members (still active in the system, gone quiet)
**Source segment:** Informz — current members with engagement score below B threshold
**Sequence:** 3 emails over ~12 days
**Status:** Draft, ready for review

---

**Subject lines (A/B test):**
- What you've been missing at FMS, [First Name]
- A quick note on your FMS membership, [First Name]

**From name:** Jennifer Lindberg

## Review Checklist (Before Building in Informz)

- [ ] No "we miss you" language anywhere
- [ ] Email 3 is genuinely plain text — no HTML, no logo, no buttons
- [ ] Subject lines are specific, not generic
- [ ] Every CTA links to a real, upcoming event or calendar page
- [ ] From name on Email 3 is Alana, not "FMS Team"
- [ ] Segment confirmed in Informz before scheduling

## Informz Setup

1. Build segment: current members with engagement score in C-tier or disengaged
2. Build Email 1, schedule send date/time
3. Build Email 2 as follow-up to non-openers of Email 1 (+5 days)
4. Build Email 3 as follow-up to non-openers of both prior emails (+7 days from Email 2)
5. Test send to yourself before scheduling
